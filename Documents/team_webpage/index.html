Welcome to the TAB webpage!

February 8, 2012Posted by Max

 

Ever wanted to know the best Bassoon fingering to use for a passage of music but don't want to look through pages upon pages of fingerings? Well then, TAB is for you! TAB (Teaching Assistant for Bassoon) is a web application for looking up Bassoon fingerings for a note or series of notes. TAB is currently in development by a Senior Design Team from the University of Idaho. Questions or suggestions? Send us an email at cs-tab@uidaho.edu!
